About Us

We at Entrepreneurship Development Cell, Zakir Husain College of Engineering & Technology (EDC-ZHCET) work towards fabricating an entrepreneurial atmosphere and providing exposure to budding entrepreneurs and entrepreneurial enthusiasts. We work with the objective of turning 'Ideas into Action', through fostering innovation and leading imagination.
